About AuditProof.AI

We are on a mission to revolutionize SAP access audits through the power of AI and automation.

Who We Are

AuditProof.AI is a security-first AI audit platform that simplifies access log reviews for SAP systems. Whether you're an internal auditor, external consultant, or enterprise customer — our tool helps you instantly summarize risks, SoD violations, and generate tamper-proof PDF reports.

Why We Built It

Traditional audit processes are slow, error-prone, and non-scalable. With AI, we eliminate manual reviews while preserving accuracy, speed, and compliance. Our goal is to democratize SAP security intelligence for teams of all sizes.

Meet the Builder

Built by a solopreneur who understands both SAP security and modern GenAI capabilities. This project is a passion to bridge security, automation, and user-friendliness — without the need for expensive tools.
